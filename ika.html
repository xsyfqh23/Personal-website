Experience


My only experience is assisting my brother in selling his very own product,Takdak.Brand. Essentially, this is a clothing company that was established in 2021 and has received a positive response from the public. The product that he sell is something that could be worn by anybody because design is best suited for everyone regardless of gender, but it is best suited to youth and adults, though the elderly can also wear it. My brother chose high-quality fabrics for the t-shirt so that everyone can experience the kind of materials that will make the people who wear it feel more comfortable all of the time. Furthermore, the t-shirts only use high-quality clothing materials, and customer feedback allows him to constantly improve in order to achieve customer satisfaction after trying out the 'Takdak.Brand' clothes. He really take care deeply about the customers, so he decided to make the products in highest quality so that they can spend their free time trying them out with their loved ones.
